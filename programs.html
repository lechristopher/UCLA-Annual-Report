The Town and Gown Affiliates of UCLA and UCLA Diversity Programs Barbeque
In fall 2018, 56 Bruins enjoyed an afternoon barbecue and presentation by Professor Bill Purdy M.A. ʼ03, Ph.D. ʼ16. Dr. Purdy spoke of UCLA’s early history and the value that longstanding networks such as theirs bring to the university’s connection with the local community.
About Town & Gown Affiliates of UCLA:
  • Founded in 1937, The Affiliates serve as a cultural bridge that links the community to UCLA.
  • The Affiliates is the university’s oldest Town & Gown organization. “Town” symbolizes the Los Angeles community that surrounds campus and “Gown” the robes of academia’s highest honors and degrees.
  • • Network events take place over lunch and dinner, offer a window into education and feature distinguished faculty lecturers and noted speakers.
  • The Affiliates are longstanding supporters of academic scholarships for students and proudly sponsor the Best Production award at Spring Sing, a showcase of UCLA’s most talented singers, dancers and sketch comedy performers.
  • Anyone is welcome to join The Town & Gown Affiliates!

Established by the UCLA Alumni Scholars Club and now in its 15th year, the UCLA Alumni Mentor Program continues to foster meaningful relationships between UCLA alumni and current UCLA students. About VetNet: Officially recognized in 2014, VetNet supports UCLA’s veteran students, alumni, faculty, staff and the local veteran community by promoting student-veteran opportunities and connections. The network raises scholarships for student-veterans, maintains productive dialogue between the student-veteran community and university administration and raises awareness of veterans’ issues through service and advocacy within the community.

Job Ready
Students are helped to prepare for life after graduation through informational interviews, resume critiques and industry-specific webinars led by alumni experts and recruiters.
  • This virtual career preparation and advancement program harnesses the expertise and experience of the UCLA Alumni Network.
  • Live video sessions lead by alumni provide critical career insights on job search strategies, networking techniques and résumé building.
  • After completing the online modules, students and alumni are matched for a one-on-one coaching session and résumé critique.
